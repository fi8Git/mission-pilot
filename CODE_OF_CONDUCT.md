# Code of Conduct

MissionPilot aims to remain a respectful, professional, and constructive open-source project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful.
- Give constructive feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Respect privacy and security boundaries.
- Avoid spam, scraping, or unethical automation patterns.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Sharing private recruiter or candidate data.
- Encouraging scraping or platform abuse.
- Publishing secrets or credentials.
- Using the project to enable spam or deceptive outreach.

## Enforcement

The repository owner may remove content, close issues, or block contributors who do not follow these expectations.
